Cotton Plant Care Guide

Soil for Cotton Plants

Well-drained, loamy soil.

Cotton plants thrive in well-drained and loamy soil. Ensure good drainage for optimal root health.

Watering

Moderate watering, avoiding waterlogging.

Water cotton plants moderately, avoiding waterlogging, to support healthy growth and fiber production.

Sunlight

Full sun exposure.

Plant cotton in an area with full sunlight exposure for optimal photosynthesis and fiber development.

Benefits

Fiber production.

Cotton plants are valued for their fiber, used in the production of textiles and various products.

Adaptable and versatile.

Cotton is a versatile crop that adapts to various climates and can be used for a wide range of products.

Considerations

Monitor for pests like boll weevils; implement crop rotation for soil health.
